## üéØ Project Objectives

---

## üß© Business Questions Addressed
The following business questions form the core of the analysis:

1. **Quick Analysis of the Data**  
   - Total employees  
   - Average & median salary  
   - Average age and tenure  
   - Overall workforce snapshot  

2. **Information Finder (Basic)**  
   - Retrieve employee details using lookup-based logic  
   - Enable quick access to employee information  

3. **Extended Information Finder (Department-Based)**  
   - Filter and retrieve employee insights by department  
   - Department-wise employee analysis  

4. **Male vs Female Comparison**  
   - Gender-based comparison of salary and performance  
   - Workforce diversity insights  

5. **Annual Bonus Calculation**  
   - Bonus calculation based on salary and performance ratings  
   - Rule-based compensation logic

3. **Company Growth Over Time (Line Chart)**  
   - Employee growth and organizational trends  

4. **Regional Scorecard**
